Statuses

Key to statuses

Status

0 - not reviewed yet

1 - reviewed by one person

2 - reviewed by two persons that disagree

3 - reviewed by two persons - btoh und/nfi

4 - reviewed by 2 persons that agree

5 - reviewed by expert

Attribute Nunerical Values

1 - pd | public domain

2 - ic | in copyright

3 - opb | out-of-print and brittle (implies in-copyright)

4 - orph | copyright-orphaned (implies in-copyright)

6 - umall | available to UM affiliates and walk-in patrons (all campuses)

7 - world | available to everyone in the world

5 - und | undetermined copyright status

8 - nobody | available to nobody; blocked for all users

9 - pdus | public domain only when viewed in the US

Reasons Numerical Values

1 | bib | bibliographically-derived by automatic processes

2 | ncn | no printed copyright notice

3 | con | contractual agreement with copyright holder on file

4 | ddd | due diligence documentation on file

5 | man | manual access control override; see note for details

6 | pvt | private personal information visible

7 | ren | copyright renewal research was conducted

8 | nfi | needs further investigation (copyright research partially complete, and an ambiguous, unclear, or other time-consuming situation was encountered)

9 | cdpp | title page or verso contain copyright date and/or place of publication information not in bib record

10 | cip | condition review and in-print status research was conducted

11 | unp | unpublished work
