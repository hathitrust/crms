5 Periodical Info on cover/title page/verso indicates work is a serial publication or periodical, possibly an extracted volume cataloged as a monograph. Terms to watch out for: Journal; Magazine; Bulletin. Look for some indication of periodicity (e.g., seasons, months, years). The presence of a number on a work, by itself, does not qualify the work as a periodical (e.g., numbered monographic series are not likely to be registered/qualify as periodicals for purposes of copyright review. Periodicals can include separately copyrighted works. Also works registered and renewed as periodicals (or contributions to periodicals) would not be not included in the Stanford Renewal Database.
