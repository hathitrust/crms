Notes

  • You can use an asterisk (*) in a search field as a wildcard that will match zero or more characters.
    • Example: searching for volume id wu.89* will match wu.89090507310, wu.89092821453, etc....
    • Example: searching for title *trigonometric* will match "Natural trigonometric functions to seven decimal places for every ten seconds of arc"
  • You can use the <, <=, >, and >= operators before a numeric expression.
    • Example: searching for pub date <=1924 will retrieve all volumes published before or in 1924.
  • OR takes precedence over AND and NOT when you use all three search fields. So x AND y OR z is interpreted as x AND (y OR z).
